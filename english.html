PSA

In this PSA, BitEruptor describes the problem of online phishing and demonstrates how their Chrome extension, named BitEruption, works. With Isaac (hacker and international speaker(Japanese, Amharic)), Aleimah (voice), Uialii (news broadcaster), Courtney (BitEruption explainer), Jocelyn (cameraman and Taiwanese speaker), and MaiAngel (nurse and Japanese speaker #2), the BitEruptor team explains how the world can greatly benefit from their chrome extension. In the scenes, the hacker obtains information about Johnson Hospital when a nurse accidentally signs into a phishing website that mimics Gmail's website. The news broadcaster announces the unfortunate news and demonstrates that Johnson Hospital is not the only place affected by hackers. The Ethiopians, Taiwanese, and Japanese (to name a few) are victims of unfortunate phishing scams. However, after the nurse downloads BitEruptor, the extension prevents her from going to the phishing site. A BitEruption explainer describes the process of how BitEruption works to identify the phishing site. Finally, the PSA ends with the team members of BitEruptor.
